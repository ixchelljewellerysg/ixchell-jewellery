FAQ

Common jadeite questions.

What should I learn first before buying jadeite?

Begin with Type A jadeite, treatment types, jade versus jadeite, and how colour, translucency and texture affect appearance.

Is Type A jadeite better?

Type A jadeite is natural jadeite that has not been chemically treated or dyed. For collectors and serious buyers, Type A is usually the preferred starting point.

Why does jadeite look different in photos?

Jadeite reacts strongly to light, background colour, skin tone, camera exposure and viewing angle. In-person viewing is often the most reliable way to understand a piece.

Can jadeite be worn daily?

Yes. Natural Type A jadeite can be worn daily with sensible care. Avoid strong impacts, harsh chemicals and careless storage.
