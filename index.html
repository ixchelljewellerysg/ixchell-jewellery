Collector Philosophy

No two jadeite pieces are ever the same.

Jadeite is formed naturally over millions of years beneath the earth.

Even when two pieces are carved from the same stone, they will never share the same colour, translucency, texture or life.

Each piece exists only once — not reproduced, not repeated, not standardised.

Every piece is, in its own way, a quiet limited edition.

Your Visit

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome if you happen to be at The Adelphi.

However, private viewing sessions are prioritised for scheduled appointments.

To ensure a proper and uninterrupted experience, we recommend arranging your visit at least one day in advance via Instagram DM.

Why are appointments arranged through Instagram DM?

All appointments are arranged exclusively via Instagram DM, ensuring a secure and verified point of contact.

How are payments handled?

All transactions are conducted in-store. We accept Visa, MasterCard and American Express for both local and international clients.

What languages can be used in the shop?

We assist clients in English, Mandarin, Bahasa Malaysia and Bahasa Indonesia.

Common Chinese dialects can also be accommodated where possible, allowing for a more natural and comfortable viewing experience.

Language, for us, is not simply a means of communication. It allows us to understand each client more precisely, so that every piece is chosen with clarity, rather than assumption.

Do you evaluate jade not bought from your shop?

No. Out of respect for our work and clients, we only advise on our own collection.
