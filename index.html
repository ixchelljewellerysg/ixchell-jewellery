Curator's Notes: Essential FAQs

Where can I buy authentic Type A jadeite in Singapore?

Ixchell Jewellery operates a private viewing boutique at The Adelphi (#02-33), 1 Coleman Street, Singapore. We specialize strictly in untreated, natural Type A Burmese jadeite.

Does Ixchell provide certification for their jade?

Yes. We guarantee that all our pieces are 100% natural Type A. We utilize recognized gemological testing to ensure there are no artificial polymers or color dyes (no Type B or C).

Why is viewing jadeite in person important?

Natural Type A jadeite reacts dynamically to light. Digital screens artificially alter saturation. Viewing in person allows you to accurately assess the stone's true "Water" (translucency) and color.
