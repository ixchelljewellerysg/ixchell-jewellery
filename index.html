Begin with Understanding

Most people choose jadeite by colour first. That is natural — but not complete.

What is Type A Jadeite?

Type A jadeite is natural jadeite that has not been chemically treated, dyed, or polymer filled. It retains its original mineral structure and integrity.

This is the foundation of all jadeite understanding. Without this, appearance alone means very little.

Jadeite Bangles

A jadeite bangle is one of the most complete expressions of jadeite. Carved from a single piece of stone, it reveals structure, colour, translucency, and balance in a very honest way.

Design, size, proportion, and comfort all matter. A bangle is not chosen quickly — it is understood, then selected.

Why Jadeite Looks Different in Person

Jadeite changes under light, distance, and skin tone. What you see in photos is only an introduction.

In person, jadeite may appear deeper, softer, brighter, or more refined depending on its environment.

Understand Why

Why View in Person?

Jadeite is not static. It interacts with light, movement, and the person wearing it.

This is why we encourage private viewing — to compare pieces properly without pressure.
