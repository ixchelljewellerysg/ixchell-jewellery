About Ixchell Jewellery

Ixchell Jewellery is a Singapore jadeite boutique focused on natural Type A Burmese jadeite. We believe buying jadeite should come with education, clarity and confidence.

Conveniently located near City Hall MRT, Funan Mall, National Gallery Singapore and Raffles City, our boutique welcomes collectors, first-time buyers and visitors looking for a meaningful Singapore find.
