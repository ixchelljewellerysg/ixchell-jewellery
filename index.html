The Ixchell Experience

Calm, human and low-pressure.

Ixchell Jewellery is intentionally kept small, low-profile and personal. Many customers visit simply to understand jadeite more clearly before making any decision.

We believe jadeite behaves very differently under natural light, indoor lighting, skin tone and distance. This is especially important for translucent jadeite bangles and finer collector pieces.

Customers who wish to further verify jadeite are welcome to independently do so through recognised gemological laboratories in Singapore, including institutions such as Nanyang Gemological Institute at Orchard Road and other recognised authorities.
