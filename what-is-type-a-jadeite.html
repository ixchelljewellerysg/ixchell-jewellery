What Is Type A Jadeite?

Type A jadeite is natural jadeite that has not been chemically bleached, resin-filled, dyed, or artificially enhanced. For anyone choosing jadeite in Singapore, this is the first question to understand before looking at colour, translucency, price, or peerless beauty.

Simple Answer

Type A means natural, not magically perfect.

Type A jadeite means the stone remains natural in its internal structure and colour. It may be cut, carved, polished and set into jewellery, but it should not be acid-bleached, polymer-filled, dyed, or artificially improved to imitate higher quality jadeite.

FAQ

Questions people ask about Type A jadeite

Is Type A jadeite the same as real jade?

Type A jadeite is real natural jadeite. However, “jade” can also refer to nephrite, which is a different mineral. Not all jade is jadeite, and not all jadeite is Type A.

Is Type A jadeite always expensive?

No. Price depends on colour, translucency, texture, size, thickness, craftsmanship, rarity and overall beauty. Type A simply tells you the jadeite is natural and untreated.

Can Type A jadeite have flaws?

Yes. Natural jadeite may have stone lines, clouds, colour variation, mineral texture or internal structures. Natural does not mean flawless; it means the stone has not been artificially altered.

Why should I view jadeite in person?

Jadeite changes under lighting, skin tone and viewing angle. In-person viewing helps you understand colour, translucency, texture, thickness and how the piece feels when worn.
