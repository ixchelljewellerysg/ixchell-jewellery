Jade vs Jadeite

Jade is not one single mineral. Properly speaking, jade refers to two recognised minerals: jadeite and nephrite.

Jade is the family name. Jadeite is one type of jade.

The word “jade” is a cultural family name, not a specific mineral. Nephrite is historically older and known for its toughness, while Jadeite is rarer and associated with the vivid Burmese jadeite admired today.
