How to Choose Jadeite

Most people start with price. That is the first mistake.

Jadeite is not chosen by price alone. It is chosen by understanding.

When you understand what you are looking at, your decision becomes calm, clear, and confident.

Start with the right question

Instead of asking “how much is this piece?”, ask:

• What am I looking at? • Why does this piece feel different? • What makes one jadeite more valuable than another?

Jadeite is not a product. It is a combination of structure, light, and presence.

The 4 things that matter

Colour

Hue, tone, and saturation. Not all green is equal. Balance matters more than intensity.

Translucency

How light moves through the stone. This creates life, depth, and quiet glow.

Texture

Fine grain structure gives jadeite its smooth, refined appearance.

Proportion

Shape, thickness, and size determine how the piece sits with you.

Why photos are not enough

Jadeite changes under light.

A piece may look bright in one setting, and soft in another.

This is why serious collectors always view jadeite in person.

What you feel matters just as much as what you see.

The right mindset

Do not rush jadeite.

The right piece does not need to convince you. It will stay in your mind quietly.

Some pieces are bold. Some are subtle. Some are meant for daily wear. Some are meant to be kept.

Your role is not to chase beauty. Your role is to recognise it.

View jadeite properly

The best way to understand jadeite is to see it in person.
