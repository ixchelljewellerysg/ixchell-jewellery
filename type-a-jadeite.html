What Is Type A Jadeite

Not all jadeite is natural. And not everything described as jade remains in its original state.

In the jadeite world, one term quietly defines everything: Type A.

What “Type A” Actually Means

Type A jadeite is completely natural and untreated.

It has only undergone cutting, shaping and polishing.

Nothing has been added. Nothing has been removed.

It remains exactly as it formed over millions of years beneath the earth.

Why Natural Matters

Jadeite is not just visual. It is structural.

Natural jadeite retains its internal integrity and stability over time.

This is what gives Type A jadeite its long-term confidence and value.

The Difference You Cannot See Immediately

Treated jadeite can appear brighter, cleaner and more perfect.

But jadeite is not meant to be perfect.

Natural jadeite carries depth, variation and quiet character.

Why Serious Buyers Begin Here

Experienced buyers do not begin with colour.

They begin with one question:

Is it Type A?

Closing

In jadeite, the most important quality is not always visible at first glance.

But once understood, everything changes.
