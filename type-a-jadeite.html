What Is Type A Jadeite?

Type A jadeite is natural jadeite that has not been chemically bleached, resin-filled, dyed, or artificially enhanced. For anyone choosing jadeite in Singapore, this is the first question to understand before looking at colour, translucency, price, or peerless beauty.

Simple Answer

Type A means natural, not magically perfect.

Type A jadeite means the stone remains natural in its internal structure and colour. It may be cut, carved, polished and set into jewellery, but it should not be acid-bleached, polymer-filled, dyed, or artificially improved to imitate higher quality jadeite.
