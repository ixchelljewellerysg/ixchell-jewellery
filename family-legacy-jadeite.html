Many luxury products follow trends. Natural Type A Burmese jadeite exists outside trends entirely.

The emotional meaning of jadeite

Jadeite sits close to the skin. Over time, it becomes associated with the warmth, movement and presence of the wearer.

A jadeite piece does not only carry material value. It often carries memory value.

This emotional connection is one reason why jadeite continues to remain deeply meaningful across Singapore, Malaysia, Sarawak, Sabah, Brunei, Indonesia and many Chinese families around the world.

A family heirloom is not measured only by price.
